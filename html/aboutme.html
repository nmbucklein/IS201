About Me

nmbucklein@gmail.com | 385-224-6732 | Provo, UT

Hi, my name is Nate Bucklein! I'm a 23 year old jazz musician and business student from Kansas City, Missouri who loves to sing, play guitar, fish, and of course, watch the Kansas City Chiefs. I am currently a full time L1 Canvas Support Engineer as well as a full time student. I am the owner/founder of a small vending machine company, with interests in expanding to other forms of property, and an eventual goal of investing in real estate.

I am the oldest of 2 boys, and have 3 large dogs that have my whole heart. My family means everything to me, and my drive and motivation each day is to make them proud, and to be able to provide a stable life to my future family as well.
